Polling Process and Details

Steps to Follow:

  1. Select your state from the options given below.
  2. Then after selecting the state , click on the "Parties and their Symbols" Button.
  3. Now different elected parties and their symbols will show up.
  4. Clearly understand the parties and their respective symbols and remember the symbol of the party you are supporting.
  5. Then click on the "Start Voting" Button
  6. Your vote will be valued according to the symbol that you have selected . So double check before choosing the symbols.
  7. If confused , then hover over the symbol and it will show up the name of the party it belongs to .
  8. After selecting the desired symbol . Click on the "Vote" Button.
  9. A confiirmation message will be displayed on your screen after pressing the "Vote" Button.
